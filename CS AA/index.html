Welcome!

Did you know that 5.1% of high school students in the USA dropped out during SY 2021-2022? Quality education is very important, yet it is unavailable for many people around the world. And even if it were available, many students still choose to drop out. In this website, we will delve into the factors that lead to students either dropping out or being unable to go to school. We also aim to let these students still learn through educational videos, despite not being officially enrolled to a school.

Some statistics and facts

  • According to CHEd, the dropout rate in Philippines Universities are at 35.15% during school year 2023-2024.
  • After the COVID-19 Pandemic, the literacy & basic math skills of students declined.
  • Sub-saharan Africa is the region with the lowest literacy rate in the world, with 40.1% of the population being illiterate.
  • Along with the previous fact, South Sudan is the country with the lowest literacy rate in the world, with it only being 35%.

Why are children even out of school?

There are numerous reasons why students don't have access to quality education, or choose to stop attending school. Here are examples of factors/reasons:

  • Poverty: Some children have to work at a young age, so they're unable to study at school.
  • Lack of government funding: There are no nearby public schools to enroll at on many places around the globe, including provinces here in the Philippines.
  • Academic Difficulties: Some children have a hard time learning at school, this can be caused by many things, such as learning disabilities, problems at home, etc.
  • Bullying: Being bullied, both physically and/or verbally, demotivates students and gradually makes them dread going to school, causing them to stop attending entirely.
